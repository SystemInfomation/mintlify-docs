  4. What authentication method is used? (email/password, SSO, OAuth, magic link?)
  5. What billing provider powers subscriptions? (Stripe, App Store, Play Store?)
  6. Are there free and paid tiers? What are the plan names?
  7. Is there a public status page URL?
  8. What is the primary support email or ticketing system?
  9. Are there integrations or APIs available to end users or developers?
  10. What community guidelines or safety policies exist?
  11. What data does Pals Club collect and how is it stored?
  12. What are the SLA commitments for support response times?
