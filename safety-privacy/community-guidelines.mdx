---
title: "Community guidelines"
description: "Rules and expectations for the Pals Club community"
---

Pals Club is built on the idea that everyone deserves a safe, welcoming space to connect. These guidelines set clear expectations so that every member can enjoy the platform without fear of abuse, harassment, or harmful content.

## Be respectful

Treat every member the way you would like to be treated. Disagreements are natural, but personal attacks, hate speech, and discriminatory language are never acceptable. Engage in good faith and assume positive intent.

## No harassment or bullying

Do not target, intimidate, or repeatedly contact someone who has asked you to stop. This includes sending threatening messages, posting someone's personal information, and coordinating group harassment. Any behavior intended to make another person feel unsafe will be treated seriously.

## No spam or misleading content

Do not post repetitive, unsolicited, or deceptive content. This includes chain messages, fake giveaways, phishing links, and misleading profiles. Automated or bot-driven activity that disrupts the experience for others is also prohibited.

## Protect privacy

Do not share another person's private information — such as their real name, address, phone number, or photos — without their explicit consent. Respect the boundaries others set around what they share publicly.

## Report violations

If you see something that breaks these guidelines, please report it. Reporting is confidential and helps keep the community safe.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the content or profile">
    Navigate to the post, message, or profile that violates the guidelines.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the report button">
    Select the **⋯** (more options) menu and choose **Report**. Pick the category that best describes the violation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add details">
    Provide any additional context that will help our Trust & Safety team review the report. Screenshots or links are helpful but not required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Tap **Submit report**. You will receive a confirmation, and our team will review the report — usually within 24 hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enforcement and consequences

Pals Club enforces these guidelines consistently and fairly. Depending on the severity and frequency of a violation, consequences may include content removal, a formal warning, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination.

<Warning>
  Violations may result in temporary or permanent account suspension. Repeat or severe offenses — such as threats of violence or sharing child exploitation material — will lead to immediate, permanent removal and may be reported to law enforcement.
</Warning>
