Pals Club is a community and social platform designed to help you build meaningful connections, share content, and engage with groups that match your interests. Whether you're catching up with friends or discovering new communities, Pals Club provides the tools you need to stay connected.

## Key feature areas

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profiles & connections" icon="user-group">
    Create a personalized profile and grow your network. [PROFILE_CUSTOMIZATION_OPTIONS] let you showcase who you are, while connection tools make it easy to find and stay in touch with the people who matter most.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Messaging & communication" icon="comments">
    Reach out to friends and groups with real-time messaging. Pals Club supports [MESSAGING_FORMATS] so you can communicate in the way that works best for you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Groups & communities" icon="people-group">
    Join or create groups around shared interests. Groups offer [GROUP_FEATURE_SET] to help communities thrive, from discussion threads to shared resources.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content sharing" icon="share-nodes">
    Share updates, photos, links, and more with your network. [CONTENT_TYPES_SUPPORTED] can be posted to your profile, within groups, or sent directly to connections.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notifications" icon="bell">
    Stay up to date with activity that matters to you. Notification preferences let you control alerts for [NOTIFICATION_CATEGORIES] across all your devices.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Discovery & search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Find people, groups, and content across the platform. [DISCOVERY_ALGORITHMS] surface relevant results so you can explore new connections and communities.
  </Card>
