## Keeping your software up to date

<Info>
  We regularly update Pals Club to improve performance, fix bugs, and add new features. For the best experience, always use the latest version of the app and keep your operating system and browser up to date.
</Info>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens if my device doesn't meet the minimum requirements?">
    Pals Club may still load, but you could experience slower performance, visual glitches, or missing features. We recommend upgrading your device or browser for the full experience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to install anything to use Pals Club on the web?">
    No. Pals Club runs entirely in your browser — no plugins or extensions are required. Just make sure JavaScript is enabled.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I check which version of the app I'm running?">
    Open the app and navigate to **Settings → About**. Your current app version and build number are displayed at the bottom of the screen.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
