Welcome to Pals Club! This guide walks you through everything you need to go from sign-up to your first interaction on the platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have the following:

- **A valid email address or phone number** — you'll need one to create your account
- **A supported device** — Pals Club works on [SUPPORTED_PLATFORMS]
- **An internet connection** — a stable broadband or mobile data connection is recommended

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Visit [PALS_CLUB_SIGNUP_URL] or open the Pals Club app and tap **Sign Up**. Enter your email address or phone number, choose a password, and accept the terms of service.

    <Tip>You can also sign up with [SUPPORTED_SSO_PROVIDERS] for a faster registration experience.</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your profile">
    Add a display name, profile photo, and a short bio so other members can learn about you. You can also configure your privacy settings during this step.

    - **Display name** — this is how others will see you on the platform
    - **Profile photo** — upload an image or choose from the default avatars
    - **Bio** — a brief description of your interests (up to [BIO_CHARACTER_LIMIT] characters)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the platform">
    Once your profile is ready, you'll land on the [PALS_CLUB_PRIMARY_WORKFLOW] screen. From here you can:

    - Browse trending topics and communities
    - Search for people, groups, or content
    - Discover personalized recommendations based on your interests
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect with others">
    Start building your network by sending connection requests or joining groups.

    1. Use the **Search** bar to find friends or communities
    2. Tap **Connect** or **Join** on any profile or group
    3. Send a message to introduce yourself

    <Tip>Joining a few groups early helps the platform personalize your feed faster.</Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
